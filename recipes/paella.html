Paella



Description :

paella, in Spanish cuisine, dish of saffron-flavoured rice cooked with meats, seafood, and vegetables.

Originating in the rice-growing areas on Spainâ€™s Mediterranean coast, the dish is especially associated with the region of Valencia.

Paella takes its name from the paellera, the utensil in which it is cooked, a flat round pan with two handles; paella is traditionally eaten from the pan.

Ingredients :

  • rice
  • saffron
  • chicken
  • vegetables

Steps :

  1. Flavour the pan
  2. Gently fry the rice
  3. Start to build a rich sauce
  4. Prepare the saffron
  5. Add the saffron and a high-quality fresh stock
  6. Boil for 5 minutes
  7. Add the meat or seafood and bake for 12 minutes
  8. Resist and rest for 5 minutes before serving
