Sushi



Description :

sushi, a staple rice dish of Japanese cuisine, consisting of cooked rice flavoured with vinegar and a variety of vegetable, egg, or raw seafood garnishes and served cold.

Ingredients :

  • raw fish
  • a nori sheet
  • sushi rice

Steps :

  1. Prep the Rice & Nori
  2. Spread the Rice on the Nori
  3. Add Fillings to the Bottom Quarter of the Nori
  4. Roll the Sushi Part Way
  5. Roll the Sushi the Rest of the Way
  6. Move to Cutting Board
  7. Cut the Sushi
